Definitions

Time Period

The 1-hour time period within which the data is aggregated, covering 3 months from 2016/06/01 to 2016/08/31 and excluding weekends.

Stockout Rate

The percentage of time (within the time period) during which a station is out of bikes.

Total Ride Pick-ups

Total number of ride pick-ups recorded during the time period.

Est. Displaced Pick-ups

Estimated number of pickup-ups that were displaced from the original station because it was empty during the time period. A displacement occurs when a rider whose first choice is unavailable picks up a ride from another available station instead.

Est. Lost Pick-ups

Estimated number of pickup-ups that were lost because a station was empty during the time period. A loss occurs when a rider whose preferred choices are unavailable decides not to use the system.

Displacement Destinations

The surrounding stations to which pick-ups are displaced when the original station is out of bikes.
